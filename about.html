About Us

 

Our mission…

Is to create a sustainable value with the clients and their families, by delivering oustanding services and business practices that help our clients experience safe, dependable and professional services that will enhance their quality of life i the confronts of their own home.

 

Our staff…

The staff at Helping Hands Home Services is committed to providing exceptional service in your home where you feel most comfortable, independent, and safe. Our team is committed to treating every client with optimal care, dignity, respect, and extreme confidentiality.

 

We realize every client's need is different, therefore we will work hand in hand with you and your family to create a personalized care plan. We are committed to investing the time and resources to hire the most qualified, professional, and passionate Personal Care Aides in the industry to help henhance your quality of life.

 

All of your healthcare professionals are certified and/or licensed by the state of Virginia. Our agency is licensed, insured and bonded. We carefully screen every prospective employee before hiring. Each applicant will undergo an extensive background check, professional reference check, multiple interviews, and pass a competency test to ensure they are qualified to provide the exceptional care and dedication our clients deserve.

The Right Choice

When making important decisions about your care or the care of loved ones; making the right choice can be tough. Let us help!

 

Our personal experiences help us understand caring for an aging or disabled loved one is one of the most significant challenges life can present. The preocess can be emotional, overwhelming and, at times, confusing. We are here for you, willing to extend a helping hand during the entire process.

 

Although it is never easy, our job is to lend that helping hand to make the load lighter and the process a little easier.
